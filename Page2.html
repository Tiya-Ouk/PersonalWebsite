My Obession for Sneakers

Pictured above is a retro jordan clock. As you can see each retro number is coordinated with its time slot, for example the retro 11 is at the 11 o'clock slot. This is one of my favorite ideas because it combines interior design with streetwear fashion. What helps me keep my passion for sneakers is my parents. Both my step dad, dad, and mom enjoy sneakers as much as I do! At least once a year we give each other a new pair of retros. We even have some matching pairs that we all bought together.

Some things that I enjoy watching are shoe reviews. Usually when there is a shoe review video the youtuber making the video has received the shoe a few days before the actual release date. Of course the show is being reviewed for things like colorways, material, comfort and the on foot visualization. The youtubers of these videos tend to do giveaways of the product they received. A lot of them are also sponsored by Champs, Footlocker, Finishline, etc; so the youtubers get to review early released products.
